About us

GreenLeaf Eco Solutions is a sustainability-driven startup founded in 2020.

We specialize in providing eco-friendly packaging, renewable energy consulting, and waste management solutions for small and medium-sized businesses. Our goal is to help communities and companies adopt greener practices without compromising on efficiency or cost.


Our Vission

To create a world where sustainable living is not a choice but a simple, everyday practice.

We envision cities, companies, and households reducing their environmental footprint while still enjoying modern conveniences.

Our Mission

  • Deliver affordable eco-friendly solutions for businesses and communities.
  • Educate and empower people about sustainable practices.
  • Innovate continuously to design products and services that protect our planet for future generations.

History

  • 2021 – Founded by two environmental engineers with a passion for clean technology.
  • 2022 – Launched our solar consultancy service and expanded operations to three cities.
  • 2023 – Recognized with the “Green Startups Award” for impactful sustainability initiatives.
  • 2025 (Today) – Serving over 500 clients nationwide, we continue to grow with the same commitment to the environment.
